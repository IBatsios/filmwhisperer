About Us

We are a full-service video production company based in Greensboro NC, with clients across the United States.

Our clients include: Golf Channel, Club Car, Stanley Furniture, Furniture Today, Mission Health Systems, a locally-based international transportation company as well as local and regional businesses and individuals.

We bring a cinematic approach to our work and approach each new project with the care and attention to detail afforded to a blockbuster movie.

We offer the whole range of services every production needs: writing, producing, directing, shooting, editing and effects. We gladly collaborate with our clients or can take an idea and shape it on our own.

Need motion graphics? We can do that, too. The perfect placement of a 3D graphic or logo can be just the right touch to set your video apart from the competition.

In addition to our productions services, we also offer training for your in-house video crew. Need to get caught up on newer technology or software advances? We can show you how to maximize the gear and applications you use.

We use Apple computers, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Creative Suite and over 25 years of experience to create exceptional content for you.
